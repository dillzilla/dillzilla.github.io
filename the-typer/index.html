How To Play

You must prove your worth by out-typing the machine.

Complete 3 sentences faster than the system's progress bar to advance to the next level.

Each level increases sentence complexity and the required typing speed (WPM).

If the system's progress bar reaches the end before you finish typing a sentence, you lose control.

Accuracy counts, but speed is paramount.

Good luck, operator.
